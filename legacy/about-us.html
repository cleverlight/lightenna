About us

Lightenna was founded in 2004 to deliver technically sound IT solutions to small UK companies.

What's the we/I business about?

Lightenna is a small company. It's just two of us at the moment. Alex is the MD and Arty's a Drupal developer. On the site, when speaking as a company, we're a we. Alex publishes a blog which is an I thing but hopefully not too egocentric!

We work in a variety of areas, essentially providing IT services: we help companies better manage, distribute and monetarise their information, principally through the web.

All our development work is based in London in Ealing.

Some of the projects we've done for UK companies are viewable in our recent work section.

If you'd like more information, please contact us.
