Database training

Businesses can be overwhelmed by a torrent of information, and may not have anyone inside the organisation responsible for managing it.

Our Database training courses empower your staff to manage that information across the enterprise.

  • 1. Data models
  • 2. DBMS Systems
  • 3. Database Design
  • 4. Distributed systems
  • 5. Real world

The courses are geared toward employees in technical roles. We look closely at what your needs are as a working community and help upskill a member of your team to meet those needs.

The course is taught by Alex Stanhope, who teaches a similar unit for Bristol University, Computer Science department.

For more information about the course please get in touch.
